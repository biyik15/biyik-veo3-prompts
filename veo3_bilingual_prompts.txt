Prompt 1 – Doa Pagi Hari / Morning Prayer

[Bahasa Indonesia]
Seorang pria Asia Tenggara berusia 50-an yang santai dan percaya diri, mengenakan peci hitam dan baju batik. Ia duduk di ruangan kecil yang penuh dengan buku, kitab, pena, dan kaligrafi Arab di dinding. Ia menatap kamera, tersenyum, lalu berdoa dengan gaya humoris dan santai:
"Ya Allah, berkahilah hariku ini, jangan biarkan aku lupa narik napas karena sibuk mikirin utang!"
Pencahayaan netral siang hari, gaya sinematik, medium close-up, ekspresi natural dan tenang.

[English]
A relaxed and confident Southeast Asian man in his 50s, wearing a black cap and batik shirt. He sits in a small room filled with books, Islamic texts, pens, and Arabic calligraphy on the wall. He looks into the camera, smiles, and delivers a humorous morning prayer:
"O Allah, bless my day. And please… don’t let me forget to breathe just because I'm busy thinking about my debts!"
Soft daylight, cinematic tone, medium close-up shot, natural and calm expressions.

Prompt 2 – Nasihat Siang Hari / Midday Advice

[Bahasa Indonesia]
Pria Asia Tenggara usia 50-an, mengenakan batik dan peci hitam, duduk santai di ruangan kecil berisi kaligrafi Arab, kitab-kitab, dan alat tulis. Ia berbicara langsung ke kamera dengan nada hangat dan bersahabat sambil tersenyum:
"Ingat ya, kalau hati kita penuh amarah… itu bukan hati lagi, itu panci presto!"
Framing medium-close-up, pencahayaan siang natural, ekspresi ramah dan penuh canda.

[English]
A Southeast Asian man in his 50s, wearing a batik shirt and a black cap, sits comfortably in a small room with Arabic calligraphy, books, and pens around him. He talks to the camera with a friendly and warm tone, smiling:
"Remember, if your heart is full of anger… maybe it’s not a heart anymore, but a pressure cooker!"
Medium-close-up framing, soft natural daylight, relaxed and humorous expression.

Prompt 3 – Doa Malam / Night Prayer

[Bahasa Indonesia]
Seorang pria Asia Tenggara berusia 50-an mengenakan batik dan peci hitam duduk di ruangan kecil bernuansa islami. Terdapat kaligrafi Arab, kitab, dan meja penuh pena. Ia menatap kamera dengan wajah damai, lalu berdoa:
"Ya Allah, jika hari ini aku belum banyak berbuat baik… semoga Engkau masih catat aku niat baiknya saja dulu."
Cahaya sore hari yang hangat, suasana tenang, framing medium-close-up, gaya visual sinematik.

[English]
A Southeast Asian man in his 50s wearing a batik shirt and black cap sits in a small, Islamic-themed room with Arabic calligraphy, religious books, and pens. He looks calmly at the camera and prays with a gentle smile:
"O Allah, if I haven’t done much good today… please at least count the good intentions I had."
Warm afternoon lighting, peaceful mood, medium-close-up shot, cinematic visual style.
